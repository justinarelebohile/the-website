HOW IT ALL STARTED

Once in a while i had a good life, good reputation and everything i needed as as a child but that did not last for long, eventualy my family got involved in a plane crash and they all couldn't make it>That was the time that life changed for me. I began to sought for ways to keep life going so i did petty jobs so that i could feed my body. That is how I became a hustler.

my story to tell

I wish I had a perfect life, perfect family and perfect friends to rely on but that only seemed so far from me for I had nothing of such but only my life to think of. For a living, I had to wake up early in the morning and ran errands for other villagers so that they could least provide with something to eat. I have to hustle to make sure that i survive no matter what the costs were,"I HAD TO HUSTLE IN OTHER TO LIVE"
